COMPARISON

A similar application is the "AtHome Camera App". This section compares the features of the "AtHome Camera" application with those of the proposed application.

ABOUT

"Athome Video Streamer(AVS)" with the "Athome Camera: Remote Monitor" application can be used together by users to watch and talk with their family anytime, anywhere.

PROS
  • Videos can be stored on a cloud storage provided by AtHome cloud.
  • Its Remote Monitoring App can be connected to CCTV Set-Top Boxes.
  • Supports two way audio.
CONS
  • AtHome App requires two different applications to be installed, one as the camera called Athome Camera: Streamer, and the other called Athome Camera: Remote Monitor.
  • Freemium versions of applications provide limited usages, forcing the user to pay for usage.
  • AtHome Camera requires an internet connection.
ADVANTAGES OF "LAPHA" OVER "ATHOME CAMERA"
  • The same application is used for camera and monitoring, so devices can be interchanged easily.
  • The application wouldn't require an internet connection to work, although the two devices must be on the same network.
