Pain Points

John Smith is a very friendly and introvert person who is not comfortable having CCTV cameras in some areas of his house, e.g. his bedroom. he understand there's need for security, but feels his privacy is more important. he's now on the lookout for an easy to setup security camera, that he can use only when the need arises and also a camera that he can be aware of and not forget to switch of when he should.

To resolve this, he downloaded the Lapha application as a solution to his problem, He will perform the following action:

  • Download the Lapha app on two devices
  • When he's not goin to be in the private place he needs to secure, then he ensures the two devices are on the network.
  • Open Lapha on the device to be used as camera, and select the camera mode from the two options.
  • Open Lapha on the second device to be used for monitoring, and select the guardian mode from the two options.
  • It would ask for a unique number to be entered for auhtentication, This unique number can be accesssed from the camera device, by clicking on the connect icon.
  • He can start monitoring the private location.
